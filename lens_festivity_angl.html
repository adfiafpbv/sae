about us


LensFestivity offers you its premises to be able to fulfill your greatest wishes

Indeed LensFestivity has two large hangars that can accommodate more than a hundred people each, perfect for all types of activities.

This reception capacity makes it possible to satisfy much more demand concerning a large gathering of people such as companies or large families.

LensFestivity allows you to make the most of the activities you will be able to do with us

Thanks to our pre-defined modules we can fit out large spaces easily and quickly

LensFestivity also offers a wide range of activities such as weddings, seminars, birthdays but as many other temporary activities like airsoft or even escape games that will be offered throughout the year and will be new and different depending on the season or the desires of different customers
